Working style

How I work best

I bring strong creative energy to projects, especially when the goal, audience, format, and production steps are clearly defined. With a clear brief, I can turn concepts into visuals, scripts, layouts, campaigns, and multimedia pieces.

Honest growth area

I work best when communication is direct and expectations are specific. When the steps are unclear, I can explore too many creative directions at once, so I value briefs that define the goal, priorities, and next actions clearly.
